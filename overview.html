01 — Who We Are

Two people who really love
each other — but struggle to show it
in the same way.

Aditya

What I Do · What I Need

  • When things are good between us, I am the most loving person I know how to be — I build things, plan things, and try to show up for you.
  • I expect that if I sacrifice time, money, and energy to come see you, that time should be ours — not interrupted by anything else.
  • In my heart, our future is already decided. I've even told you many times. I just need you to be ready too.
  • When something scares me — like you getting close to someone at work — I don't always say it clearly. It comes out as anger instead of just saying "I'm scared."
  • I really need your words and actions to match. Every time they don't, it hurts more than you know.
  • I want loyalty to feel like a daily choice you make — not just a promise to stop me from leaving.
  • I am at my best when I feel chosen by you. I am at my worst when I feel like just an option.

Esita

What You Do · What You Need

  • You stayed — through every fight, every ultimatum, and every "I'm blocking you." That shows me your feelings are very real.
  • You are figuring out who you are right now — away from your family, in a new city, with a new job. I know that is hard to balance but I am always trying to understand and support you(In my way).
  • You struggle to handle what your family expects and what I need at the exact same time. You are still learning how to balance both. But you always choose them over me.(even i am standing right in front of you)
  • When a fight gets too big, you want to end as quick as possible and promise to be better but you never do.
  • You said it beautifully once: "Before coming into this relationship I was not the person I am now… now I know because I love you." That felt so real to me.
  • You need patience. You need me not to get angry when you make mistakes while you are still figuring things out.

"I just want you to be happy. I just want you to be with me. I just want you to love me. I just want you to choose me. I just want you to be mine."

— Esita

"Two people who genuinely love each other — and keep proving it in incompatible ways."

— This is who they are beneath all the noise.
